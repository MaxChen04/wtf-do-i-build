# Readiness gate

Mark a lead ready only when there is a specific person or workflow, a painful or repeated moment, and enough direct evidence to explain why this user should explore it now. A vague wish for “an AI app” or “a portfolio project” is not ready.

When not ready, write one concrete assignment that creates evidence: observe five instances of a workflow, interview two affected people, keep a one-week friction log, or reproduce the current workaround. Explain what the assignment will reveal and when to return. Ask one repair question at a time; do not exceed three repair answers before prescribing the assignment.
