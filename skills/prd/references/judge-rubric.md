# PRD judge rubric

Score each dimension 0–2: earned insight, desperate specificity, why-now gap, narrow-to-adjacent scope, builder-shippable v1, and demand pull. Also check grounding, portability, resumability, ADR reasoning, Mermaid validity, and evaluation quality.

Cap a dimension at 1 when its claim lacks direct evidence. A zero blocks delivery. Pass requires at least 10/12 with no zero. After two revisions, allow `ship with flags` only at 8/12 or above with no zero and disclose the remaining risks. Prefer three to five concrete issues with section and fix over generic advice.

Reject solution-first framing, borrowed trend energy, capability-only rationale, broad platform v1s, validation theater, and a missing first success moment.
