# Direction quality gate

Every presented direction needs a specific evidence trail, a distinct first wedge, a first success moment, a primary risk, and a reason the user is unusually well placed to explore it. It must be buildable as a small project and have a credible adjacent path.

Generate materially different product shapes and user moments, not feature variants. Reject table-stakes clones, platform-shaped v1s, trend-driven wrappers, capability-only ideas, or directions that fail the swap test. Recommend the direction with the strongest direct pain, access, and smallest credible demand test.
